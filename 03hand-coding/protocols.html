Protocols

Getting Started: Beginner Protocol

This beginner protocol is designed to help you gently support your body, reduce inflammation, and build a strong foundation for long-term health.

  • Focus on Whole, Simple Foods: Choose meals made from whole ingredients—meat, eggs, fruit, vegetables, and healthy fats. Limit highly processed foods and added sugars.
  • Support Your Gut: Start your day with a gut-friendly habit like warm lemon water, fermented foods, or increasing fiber from fruits and vegetables.
  • Hydrate Well: Drink water consistently throughout the day. Add a pinch of sea salt to one or two glasses to support minerals and energy.
  • Prioritize Rest: Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep each night and keep a consistent sleep schedule.
  • Reduce Daily Stress: Practice simple stress-reducing habits like deep breathing, walking, journaling, or time outside.
  • Start Gentle Movement: Include 10–20 minutes of easy movement daily—walking, stretching, or light yoga.
  • Observe and Track Symptoms: Note changes in digestion, energy, mood, and sleep to recognize patterns over time.

Anti-Inflammatory Protocol

This protocol helps reduce chronic inflammation and supports the body’s natural healing processes.

  • Prioritize whole foods such as meat, fish, eggs, fruit, vegetables, herbs, and healthy fats.
  • Reduce processed foods, sugar, alcohol, and industrial seed oils.
  • Add anti-inflammatory foods daily: berries, turmeric, ginger, leafy greens, bone broth, and olive oil.
  • Stay hydrated and maintain stable blood sugar with balanced meals.
  • Include 20–30 minutes of gentle movement most days.

Gut Support Protocol

This protocol strengthens digestion, balances the microbiome, and supports the gut lining.

  • Increase fiber from fruits, cooked vegetables, and whole foods.
  • Include fermented foods if tolerated, such as sauerkraut or kimchi.
  • Add gut-soothing options like bone broth, aloe vera juice, or ginger tea.
  • Remove common irritants such as excess sugar, processed foods, and alcohol.
  • Eat slowly, chew well, and avoid overeating.

Hormone Support Protocol

This protocol supports balanced hormones through nutrition, stress reduction, and lifestyle habits.

  • Focus on protein-rich meals and healthy fats for stable hormones.
  • Keep blood sugar stable by eating regularly and pairing protein with fiber.
  • Reduce stress through grounding, deep breathing, or mindfulness.
  • Prioritize 7–9 hours of sleep to regulate cortisol and support recovery.
  • Add mineral-rich foods like spinach, avocado, eggs, shellfish, and sea salt.

Detox & Drainage Basics

This protocol supports the liver, kidneys, and lymphatic system while encouraging healthy drainage pathways.

  • Hydrate consistently to support kidney and lymphatic function.
  • Eat liver-supporting foods like leafy greens, beets, lemon, and cruciferous vegetables.
  • Move daily to promote lymphatic drainage—walking, stretching, or rebounding.
  • Practice detox habits such as dry brushing, sauna, or deep breathing.
  • Avoid excessive toxin exposure from processed foods, smoking, alcohol, and synthetic fragrances.
